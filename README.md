# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis & Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the Superstore sales dataset to uncover meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis focuses on identifying sales trends, profitability patterns, and the impact of discounts on overall performance.

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze sales performance by category and region
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products
- Study monthly sales trends
- Understand the relationship between discount and profit
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Top 10 Profitable Products
- Profit vs Discount Analysis
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard Creation

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- The West region generated the highest sales.
- Technology category produced the highest profit.
- High discount rates negatively impacted profitability.
- Some sub-categories consistently generated losses.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Reduce excessive discount strategies.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
- Promote profitable product categories.
- Re-evaluate loss-making products.
